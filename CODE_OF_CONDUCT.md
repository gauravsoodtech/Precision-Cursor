# Code of Conduct

DPI Assistant is intended to be a respectful, practical, and beginner-friendly project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Assume good intent, but be clear when something is technically incorrect or unsafe.
- Help keep issues and pull requests useful for maintainers and users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing someone else's private information.
- Spam, trolling, or intentionally disruptive comments.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, or private files in issues or pull requests.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
